I am Laura and I am an architect, who specialized in housing.
Sustainability and practicality are at the core of my activity, but mostly I want you to enjoy the journey.

My experience is that most people don't think they can afford an architect, or they don't even know that they need one.

However, building or renovating a house (even just 1 room) is a quite complex process that can be very stressful and overwhelming when you do it for the first time.

That is where I come in and smooth out the process for you. I can help you find the perfect layout among many options, ask for a building permit, or instruct the workers to build it. Through drawing, visualizations and 3d models I will make sure you completely visualize the space before it's done.

Every situation it's slightly different, so if you are not sure what you need, just send me a message. Always happy to hear new ideas.
